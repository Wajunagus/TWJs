Bring your ideas to life with Artifacts

Did you know Claude can create more than just text? Today, I want to introduce you to one of Claude's most powerful features: Artifacts.

What are Artifacts? Think of Artifacts as a dedicated window next to the chat. When Claude creates something - like a diagram, code, or interactive tool - it appears there, bringing your ideas and projects to life.

Here's what you can ask Claude to create:

  • Flowcharts & diagrams: Create a flowchart showing the customer journey for an e-commerce site
  • Data visualizations: Create a chart from my CSV file that compares my different monthly expenses
  • Interactive quizzes: Make an interactive quiz to help me practice for my upcoming history test
  • Coding projects: Create a simple to-do list app using JavaScript
  • Learning tools: Make flashcards to help me learn everyday Spanish phrases for my upcoming trip to Mexico City

Try this: Ask Claude to "create a decision tree to help me decide whether to buy or rent a home" and see what happens!
