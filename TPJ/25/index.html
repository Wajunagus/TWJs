EYE CARE 101 - KEEP YOUR VISION STRONG

Your eyes work hard every day, so why not give them the care they deserve?

Here are five simple ways to maintain clear, healthy vision all year long:

  • 🔹 Eat for Eye Health - A diet rich in omega-3s, vitamins A, C, and E, and antioxidants helps protect your vision. Leafy greens, fish, and citrus fruits are your eyes' best friends!
  • 🔹 Protect Your Eyes from Screens - The 20-20-20 rule can help reduce digital eye strain: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds. Consider blue-light blocking lenses for extra protection.
  • 🔹 Wear Sunglasses - Even on Cloudy Days - UV rays can harm your eyes year-round. A pair of 100% UV-blocking sunglasses keeps your vision sharp and shields against damage.
  • 🔹 Stay Hydrated & Blink More - Dry eyes? It might be dehydration or too much screen time. Drink plenty of water and blink frequently to keep your eyes moisturized.
  • 🔹 Get Regular Eye Exams - Even if your vision seems fine, a routine eye test ensures early detection of any issues. Book your comprehensive eye exam today!
